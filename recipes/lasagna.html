Lasagna

Description

This dish will fully serve up to 4 people. One of the best Lasagna recipes that I found during my voyages.

This recipe is easy to follow and is a good starting point for any beginner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Bologna
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in milk
  2. Cook the bolognona
  3. Mix the tomato sauce in the bologna
  4. In a recipient, make layers of pasta and sauce
  5. Finish with the cheese
  6. Put in the oven for 1 hour
